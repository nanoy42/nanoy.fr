DinoMail is a small Django app to interface with Postfix and Dovecot to manage a Mail server.

It has the following functions :

* Create, modify and delete domains
* Verify DKIM, DMARC and SPF on domains
* Create, modify and delete users.
* Handle users' password
* Create, modify and delete aliases.
